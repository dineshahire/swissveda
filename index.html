The Origin

Born of the Alps,
perfected by patience.

High in the Swiss valleys, free-roaming herds graze on wild alpine grasses. Their milk carries a richness that machines can't hurry — so we don't. Every batch of VK Swiss is slow-simmered the old way, until nothing remains but pure, golden clarity.

Inside the Jar

02

03

Sealed at Source

Hand-poured and sealed warm, locking in the nutty aroma and golden hue.
